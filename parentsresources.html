Parents Resources

FAQ

We are open Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.

You can schedule a tour by contacting us through our website’s contact form or by calling us at 7778889999.

Our ratio follows the provincial guidelines: 1 caregiver per 4 infants, 1 per 8 toddlers, and 1 per 12 preschoolers.

We offer programs for Infants (6 months to 18 months), Toddlers (18 months to 3 years), Preschool (3 to 5 years), and After-School care (5 to 12 years).

You can start the enrollment process by filling out our online registration form under the "Parent Resources" page or by contacting us directly.

Yes, we maintain a waitlist for our programs. If a spot is not immediately available, we will notify you as soon as one opens.

Each age group has a tailored curriculum focused on cognitive, social, and emotional development. Visit our "Programs" page for more details.

We follow strict health protocols, including daily sanitation of toys and equipment, regular hand washing, and maintaining a secure environment. Our staff is CPR and First Aid certified.

For the well-being of all children, we ask that sick children remain home. Children must be fever-free for 24 hours before returning to daycare.

Our fees vary depending on the program. You can find detailed information about our fees on the "Parent Resources" page or by contacting us.
