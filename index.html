Welcome to Valley View Daycare, where every day is a new adventure in learning, growing, and playing! Nestled in a nurturing environment, we provide a safe and supportive space where your child’s curiosity and creativity can flourish. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to fostering a love of learning through fun, interactive programs designed to develop both academic and social skills. Family Guide

Child Development Programs

Infant Program (0–12 months)

Focus on nurturing and care, including feeding, diaper changes, and regular naps.
Stimulate early sensory and motor development with activities like tummy time, singing, and soft toys.
Individualized care plans for each infant based on their specific needs and routines.

Toddler Program (1–3 years)

Language Development: Activities like storytime, music, and simple vocabulary games to encourage early speech and communication skills.
Fine and Gross Motor Skills: Structured play like climbing, crawling, and crafts to develop coordination and motor skills.

Our Approach

At Valley View Daycare, we believe in creating a nurturing and stimulating environment where every child feels safe, supported, and inspired to grow. Our experienced staff is dedicated to providing a balance of structured learning and creative play, fostering both educational development and social-emotional growth. From infants to preschoolers, we offer personalized care tailored to each child's unique needs, ensuring they thrive in a space that feels like home. With a focus on early childhood education, community values, and building strong partnerships with parents, we are committed to helping children reach their fullest potential.
