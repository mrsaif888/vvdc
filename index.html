Child Development Programs

Infant Program (0–12 months)

Focus on nurturing and care, including feeding, diaper changes, and regular naps.
Stimulate early sensory and motor development with activities like tummy time, singing, and soft toys.
Individualized care plans for each infant based on their specific needs and routines.

Toddler Program (1–3 years)

Language Development: Activities like storytime, music, and simple vocabulary games to encourage early speech and communication skills.
Fine and Gross Motor Skills: Structured play like climbing, crawling, and crafts to develop coordination and motor skills.
